Pizza Recipe

Easy Pizza Recipe:

Here is an interesting take on Pizza. This recipe shouldn't be too hard for beginners and it only takes a total of 30 mins to make! Enjoy!

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (10oz) refrigerated pizza crust doe
  • 1 cup hummus spread
  • 1 and 1/2 sliced bell peppers
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 2 cups shredded Montery Jack cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 475 degrees F (220 degrees C)
  2. Roll out pizza crust and place on pizza pan or baking sheet
  3. Spread thin layer of hummus over crust
  4. Arrange sliced peppers and broccoli over the hummus and top with shredded cheese.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until the crust is gold brown and the cheese is melted in the center, 10 to 15 mins. Slice and serve.
