Ramen Noodle Recipe:

This is a recipe I found through the Odin Project. It looks really interesting because it uses all natural ingredients!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup vegtable broth
  • 1 cup water
  • 3/4 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 3/4 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 3/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 2 ounces soba noodles

Steps:

  1. Boil water with soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, and hot sauce
  2. Add noodless
  3. Cook until noodles are tender/firm. 5 to 7 min.
  4. Transfer noodles to bowl with desired amount of broth
