Devil Eggs-Mexican Style!

Here is an Easy Devil Egg Recipe:

Here is a recipe of Devil Eggs but with a bit of a Mexican twist! Please enjoy this recipe and share it it with your friends and family! Cheers!

Ingredients:

  • 12 Hard-cooked eggs
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup yellow mustard
  • 1/4 cup chopped sweet pickles
  • 1/4 cup diced red onions
  • 1/2 avocado-peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 1/2 seeded and dried jalepeno pepper
  • salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground chipotle chile pepper

Steps

  1. Slice eggs in half and scoop yolks into a bowl
  2. Mash yolks with a fork and stir mayonaise, mustard, sweet pickles, red onion, avocado, jalapeno pepper, salt, and black pepper until thoroughly combined.
  3. Spoon fill the filling into the egg halves
  4. Sprinkle each deviled egg half with a pinch of ground chipotle chile
